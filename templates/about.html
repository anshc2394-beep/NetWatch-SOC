Core Methodology

Unlike traditional Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) that rely on blacklisted IP addresses or static packet signatures, NetWatch AI operates on a Zero-Knowledge Baseline.

The system assumes that 95-99% of your traffic represents valid business operations. By training an Isolation Forest model during the calibration phase, we establish a high-dimensional "normality envelope." Any subsequent flow that exists in a sparse region of this envelope is flagged as a mathematical anomaly.

Key Scenarios

  • Exfiltration Detection: Unusual byte-to-packet ratios on encrypted channels.
  • Reconnaissance: Rapid, low-duration flows targeting sequential port ranges.
  • C&C Heartbeats: Highly stable Inter-Arrival Times (IAT) that deviate from human-driven jitter.
